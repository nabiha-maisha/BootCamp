Do you know what is a balanced diet?

Firstly, what is a diet? Anything you eat is a diet and inorder to be healthy, a person must have a balanced diet.A balanced diet is a diet that contains different kinds of foods in certain quantities and proportions so that the requirement for calories, proteins, minerals, vitamins and alternative nutrients is fulfilled. When the requirement of the body is fulfilled, it works properly and the person acheives a healthy lifestyle.

A balanced diet can be maintained using a Food Pyramid. You may ask, What is a Food Pyramid?

A Food Pyramid shows the correct and balanced proportion of the various food groups that are necessary for a person. A Food Pyramid includes:

  • PROTEINS
  • VITAMINS
  • CARBOHYDRATES
  • FATS
  • MINERALS
  • FIBRES
